# 01 Brand And Positioning

## Brand Name

Hakan Leonardo

## Tagline

Yapay Zekâ Sistemleri ve İş Ajanları

## Brand Role

Hakan Leonardo should feel like a serious AI systems partner, not a generic chatbot vendor.

The brand promises clarity, system design, operational intelligence, and guided implementation for companies that want AI agents but do not yet know the right path.

This public website should not position itself as Leonardo Tales Core, Dev, Science, an agent OS, or a software factory. Those future projects should live in separate repositories.

## Initial Audience

- Turkish founders and operators.
- Service businesses that receive repetitive customer questions.
- Teams that manually prepare sales proposals or quotes.
- Businesses with operational workflows that can be automated.
- Companies that want AI but need diagnosis before buying implementation.

## Positioning Boundaries

Hakan Leonardo is not positioned as:

- A cheap chatbot widget.
- A marketplace of generic prompts.
- A broad enterprise platform on day one.
- A pricing-first SaaS checkout flow.
- The final Leonardo Tales Core/Dev/Science project.
- A public agent operating system platform.

Hakan Leonardo is positioned as:

- A serious AI discovery and implementation studio.
- A practical AI systems and business-agent service interface.
- A practical route from business diagnosis to agent deployment.

## Product Ladder

1. AI Müşteri Temsilcisi + Website - first sellable product.
2. AI Satış ve Teklif Sistemi - second product.
3. AI Operasyon Otomasyonu - third product.

## Wordmark

Keep the logo/wordmark text-based for now as `Hakan Leonardo`.

Do not introduce a symbol-heavy or illustrative logo unless a later brand sprint explicitly requests it.

## Domain

Public domain target should be finalized before deployment work resumes. Do not change deployment settings during this repositioning sprint.
